# Content and Media Checklist

## A. Must-have launch materials

### Brand and contact
- Final English company name
- Logo in SVG and PNG
- Correct sales email
- Phone and WhatsApp
- Factory address
- Company registration details that may be published

### Homepage factory video
- Aerial or high-angle exterior footage
- Workshop wide shot
- Conductor stranding / insulation / armour / sheathing footage
- Cable drum and finished-product footage
- Quality inspection footage

### Core product photos
At least 5–8 usable images for each:
1. Low Voltage Armoured Power Cables
2. Medium Voltage Power Cables
3. LSZH & Fire-safe Cables

Preferred image types:
- Clean product cross-section
- Finished cable/drum
- Production process
- Close-up of marking
- Packing and shipment
- Laboratory/test scene

### Technical evidence
- Verified product datasheets
- Applicable certificates and test reports
- Equipment list
- Standard production range table
- Routine test capabilities
- Packaging/drum specification

## B. Product data required for each individual page

- Commercial English product name
- Internal/Chinese model
- Voltage rating
- Standard
- Conductor material and class
- Number of cores
- Cross-section range
- Insulation
- Screen
- Bedding
- Armour
- Outer sheath
- Operating temperature
- Installation/application
- Available flame/fire/LSZH options
- Routine/type test references
- Datasheet PDF
- Product photos

## C. Project case template

- Country and project sector
- Customer name only when publication is permitted
- Project date
- Supplied product
- Voltage/model/standard
- Quantity or scope (optional)
- Huanyu's role
- Project problem and supplied solution
- Approved photographs/documents

## D. Content rule

Do not publish:
- Unverified capacity numbers
- Standards not actually producible/testable
- Customer logos without permission
- AI-generated technical claims without engineering review
- Generic news articles unrelated to products or buyer questions
